By Car:
From Mumbai:
From Eastern Express Highway, take the Airoli Exit and head towards Airoli on Mulund-Airoli Bridge. As you cross the Toll Plaza, take the first left (Diva Chauk, Ramdas Swami Road). Take the second left (landmark: Ganapati Temple). Two more left turns would take you to the hall.

From Thane/Pune:
From Thane-Belapur Road, take the exit to Airoli-Mulund Bridge Road and take the right at Diva Chauk (Ramdas Swami Road). Take the second left (landmark: Ganapati Temple). Two more left turns would take you to the hall.


By Bus:
Get down at Garam Masala Hotel bus stop and walk backwards towards Mulund. Take the first right (small undeveloped road). You will reach the hall in 2 minutes.



By Local Train:
From Airoli Station, you can either walk (15/20 mins) or take an auto for Gujarati Hall (Rs. 20/25).
